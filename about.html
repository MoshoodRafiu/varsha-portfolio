About Me

Varsha Thampi is a Product Manager currently working in a fast-paced start-up helping the company with product ideation, technical development, and successful product launches. As a product enthusiast, she is passionate about creating innovative solutions that make someone's life easier.

Varsha graduated from the University of Mumbai, where she did her Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics & Telecommunications, while she started her career in the technical field, her final year engineering project is what made her realize that she had a stronger management skill that she had started enjoying. This motivated her to go for higher studies and she gracefully secured MBA degree in Product Management & Operations. She likes to keep herself updated and meet, interact, and learn from other great product managers in the industry, so she joined product school which enhanced her skills even more. Since 2013, she has been providing success to both start-up and midsized firms as a Product Manager.

When not developing new product ideas, Varsha can be seen spending quality time with her friends and family. Being a creative person, she loves DIY arts/crafts and simply enjoys decorating her home with little and big things. She doesn’t have a sweet tooth but her love for her favorite food (Chocolate pastries) has no bound. One fun fact about her is – she has this obsessive need to keep her home clean and that's why she is described as too organized by her family, friends, and colleagues.
