Who I Am

I am a product enthusiast whose passion for products lead me towards my profession of Product Manager. I love creating innovative solutions that make someone's life easier and efficient. Being a Product Manager came naturally to me as I love talking to people, and communication is definitely one of the key elements of a successful Product Manager.

Since graduating from the University of Mumbai with an MBA in Product Management & Operations, I have been helping start-ups and medium businesses with product ideation, technical development, and product launch.

When not thinking about improving products, I like to channelize my inner creative persona to decorate my home using DIY art and craft. Other than that, I enjoy good food in good company.

Where I Work

I’m a Product Manager at Contiq. We help the sellers simplify the B2B buying process by integrating information silos, giving real-time insights on their deals, help them prfioritize which deal needs more attention and creating a “single pane of glass” view for all seller-buyer interactions. How I Work

Research

Understand the problem, empathize with the customer. Explore the competitive landscape. Identify strategic opportunities. Define design goals and requirements.

Design

Combine effective interfaces, smart interactions, and consistent visual language into a captivating design that resonates with the target audience.

Build

Help the developers to take the design to code. Smoothen constraints. Solve edge cases. Fill the gap between functional and delightful.

Refine

Test prototypes with real people against actual problems. Combine insights with gut intuition and experience. Iterate and improve in pursuit of perfection.
